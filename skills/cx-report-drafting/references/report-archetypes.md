# CX Stars Report Archetypes

## 1. Company Deep-Dive
Focus: single company performance across all four pillars.
- Executive Summary (company overview, overall rating, band)
- Pillar Breakdown (Digital, Service, Brand, Employee — each with score + evidence)
- Strengths and Best Practices (top 3-5 standout areas with qualitative evidence)
- Areas for Improvement (bottom 3-5 criteria with specific gaps)
- Peer Comparison (vs. industry average, vs. top performer)
- Recommendations (actionable steps based on gap analysis)

## 2. Industry Overview
Focus: benchmarking across all companies in a single industry.
- Executive Summary (industry landscape, company count, average scores)
- Industry Leaderboard (ranked table with overall and pillar scores)
- Pillar Analysis (which pillar is strongest/weakest for the industry)
- Best Practices Spotlight (top 3 practices from industry leaders)
- Industry Trends (common themes, emerging patterns)
- Cross-Industry Comparison (how this industry compares to others)

## 3. National Report
Focus: cross-industry analysis for a single country.
- Executive Summary (national CX landscape, company/industry counts)
- National Scorecard (average scores across pillars)
- Industry Comparison (which industries lead, which lag)
- Top Performers (leaderboard across all industries)
- Best Practices by Pillar (national-level best practices)
- Recommendations (national-level CX improvement priorities)
