# CX Report Drafting

## When to use
- "Draft a report on the Banking industry"
- "Write a deep-dive on Company X"
- "Generate the Digital Experience section"
- "Create a national CX report for Singapore"

## Report Archetypes
Select the appropriate archetype — see [references/report-archetypes.md](references/report-archetypes.md)

## Process
1. Identify the archetype (Company Deep-Dive, Industry Overview, National Report)
2. Gather data: use query_scores for quantitative facts, semantic_search for qualitative evidence
3. Follow the section structure in [references/report-structure.md](references/report-structure.md)
4. Apply the methodology context from [references/cx-methodology.md](references/cx-methodology.md)

## Writing Rules
- Third person, professional analytical tone
- 250-400 words per section
- Every claim must be backed by a data point or qualitative evidence
- Include specific company names and scores
- Use mastery band labels alongside numeric scores
- Structure: finding → evidence → implication
