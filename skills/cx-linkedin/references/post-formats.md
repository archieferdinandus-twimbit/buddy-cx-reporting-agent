# LinkedIn Post Formats for CX Stars

## Hook Styles (pick one)
1. **Data-led**: "Only 12% of Banking companies achieve Exceptional CX ratings."
2. **Contrarian**: "The best digital experience in ASEAN isn't from a tech company."
3. **Question**: "What separates a 4.5 from a 3.0 in Customer Experience?"
4. **Ranking reveal**: "We just ranked 100+ companies across 124 CX criteria. Here's what stood out."

## Post Structure
1. Hook (1-2 lines) — bold insight, no fluff
2. Context (1-2 lines) — what was studied, methodology in brief
3. Key findings (3-5 lines) — 2-3 specific, numbered insights with data
4. Insight/implication (2-3 lines) — what this means
5. CTA (1 line) — "Read the full report" / "Comment your take"
6. Hashtags (4-6) — #CX #CustomerExperience + industry/topic-specific

## Formatting Rules
- Use line breaks between sections for scannability
- Occasional emoji for visual breaks: 🔵 📊 ⚡ (sparingly, max 3 per post)
- No markdown bold/italic — LinkedIn renders plain text
- Numbers and percentages grab attention — use them early

## Twimbit Voice
- Data-driven, not opinion-driven
- Practitioner perspective, not academic
- Specific and evidence-based, not vague
- Professional but not stiff — conversational authority

## Example CTA Patterns
- "Which company surprised you most? Share in the comments."
- "Read the full CX Stars report → [link]"
- "Tag a CX leader who should see these results."
