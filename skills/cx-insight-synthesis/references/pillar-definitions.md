# CX Stars Four Pillars

## 1. Digital Experience
The quality and effectiveness of digital touchpoints and channels.
- Mobile app experience, website usability, digital self-service
- Digital onboarding, omnichannel integration
- Chatbot and virtual assistant quality
- Digital payment and transaction capabilities

## 2. Service Experience
The quality of human and automated service interactions.
- Contact centre performance, response times
- Issue resolution effectiveness, first-contact resolution
- Proactive service and outreach
- Service personalization and empathy

## 3. Brand Experience
How the brand is perceived and delivers on its promise.
- Brand consistency across touchpoints
- Brand trust and reputation
- Marketing and communication effectiveness
- Social media presence and engagement
- Corporate social responsibility

## 4. Employee Experience
How employee engagement translates to customer outcomes.
- Employee training and development
- Employee empowerment and autonomy
- Internal communication and culture
- Technology and tools provided to frontline staff
- Employee satisfaction and retention indicators
