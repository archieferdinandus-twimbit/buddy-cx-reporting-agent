# CX Stars Mastery Bands

## Score Ranges
| Band | Score Range | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Emerging | 0.0 — 1.4 | Early-stage CX capabilities. Foundational processes not yet established. |
| Developing | 1.5 — 2.9 | Basic CX infrastructure in place. Inconsistent execution across pillars. |
| Proficient | 3.0 — 3.99 | Solid CX capabilities across pillars. Consistent, reliable delivery. |
| Advanced | 4.0 — 4.4 | Industry-leading practices in multiple pillars. Innovation visible. |
| Exceptional | 4.5 — 5.0 | Best-in-class across all pillars. Sets industry benchmarks. |

## Interpretation Rules
- Always reference the band name alongside the numeric score
- When comparing companies, note the band gap (e.g., "Company A is Advanced while Company B is Developing — a two-band gap")
- Industry leaders are typically Advanced or Exceptional
- A company's band may differ across individual pillars vs. their overall CX Star Rating
